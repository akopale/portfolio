Coordinated Views for analyzing multivariate relationships

Below is a map showing how demographic variables change by different departments in Haiti. Under the map is a chart showing how the variables relate to each othe. For example, you can see that departments that have higher literacy and secondary education rates for women also tend to have lower infant mortality rates and total fertility rates.

Where "InfantMo" is infant mortality rate, "2_ed" is the percentage of women receiving a secondary education or higher, "w_lit" is the percent of literate women, and "TFR" is totally fertility rate. The column titled "DHSREGEN" has the names of the various departments.
